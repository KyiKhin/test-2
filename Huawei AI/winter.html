The places
you should visit in winter season

Mid-October – February (Winter)

This is the most popular season for tourists to travel because of the lower temperatures (evenings in the mountains can be cold –10 degrees Celsius) but the daytime temperature around most of the country is about 30 degrees. Traditionally November marks the beginning of the dry season (although November 2013 saw more rainy days then July or August of that year). Weather wise this is the perfect time to visit everywhere in the country (but bring warm clothes for Inle Lake and higher areas). The downfall is that it can be busy at the major tourist locations – it is much harder to find yourself ‘away from the crowds’ no matter how carefully we plan your itinerary.



TAUNG-GYI

Taunggyi is the capital and largest city of Shan State, Myanmar.

TAUNG-GYI

The city is famous for its hot air ballon festival held in November(on the full moon day of tazaungmon.)

TAUNG-GYI

The weather is cool and it is the most suitable place to visit in winter season.

TAUNG-GYI

The famous places in taunggyi are Shwe Phone Pwint Pagoda, Aye thar yar wine garden, and Eastern Amusement park

TAUNG-GYI

Other famous places to visit in shan state are inlay lake,kalaw, and pindaya.

Pyin Oo Lwin

Pyin Oo Lwin colloquially referred to as MayMyo, is a scenic hill town. It is located in Mandalay Region,Myanmar.

Pyin Oo Lwin

It is well known for its colonial style houses, hotels.Pine trees, differend kinds of flowers, and silver-oak abound in town.

Pyin Oo Lwin

The weather is delightfully cool and pleasant the whole year round.

Pyin Oo Lwin

The famous places are Kandawgyi botanical garden,pwe kauk fall, and peik chin myaung cave.

Pyin Oo Lwin

Other famous places to visit in Mandalay region is Mandalay, Bagan, and Moe Goak.

MyitKyiNa(Kachin)

Myitkyina is the capital city of kachin state,Myanmar. It is on the west bank of the Ayeyarwaddy river.

MyitKyiNa(Kachin)

Kachin state is well known for its abundance of jade and gold and also for having the highest mountain in Asia,Khakaborazi.

MyitKyina(Kachin)

Temperatures are very warm throughout the year, although the winter months are milder.

MyitKyina(Kachin)

Famous places are Mount khakaborazi,Myint Sone, and Pan wa village.

Kachin State

Other famous places to visit in kachin state are Phakant,Myit sone, and Putao.
